Welcome to ICACIE 2022

ICACIE 2022 focuses on all aspects of theory and applications in various areas of advanced computing and intelligent engineering. ICACIE is a multidisciplinary conference organized with the objective of bringing together academicians, scientists, researchers from industry, research scholars as well as students working in all areas of advanced computing and intelligent engineering. The conference will provide the authors along with the listeners with opportunities for national, international collaboration, networking among universities as well as institutions from Mauritius and abroad for promoting research and developing technologies. This conference's aim is to promote the translation of basic research into applied investigation and then into the practice. This conference will also create awareness about the importance of basic scientific research in different fields matching with the current trends. The conference will provide the flavor of keynote lectures by eminent speakers from different areas & panel discussion by industry people. The scope of the conference includes all areas of Advanced Computing and Intelligent Engineering.

ICACIE 2022 goes online!

Thisonference will be held online.

Due to the sanitary measures and travel restrictions related to the coronavirus pandemic, the chairs of the ICACIE2022 Conference have decided to hold this year’s conference as an online-only event.

Some important matters relating to the virtual conference of ICACIE 2022 are as follows:

  • All accepted papers will be published by Springer in its LNNS series.
  • Paper submission deadline and important dates stay the same.
  • The virtual conference is programmed and adapted to better suit time differences, online presentation, and discussion formats.
