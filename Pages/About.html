About ICACIE 2022

ICACIE 2022 focuses on theory as well as applications in the broad areas of advanced computing and intelligent engineering. ICACIE is a multidisciplinary conference organized to bring together academicians, scientists, researchers from industry, research scholars along with students working in all areas of advanced computing and intelligent engineering. The conference will provide the authors and listeners with opportunities for global collaboration as well as networking among institutions from all around the world for promoting research along with developing technologies. This conference aims to promote the translation of basic research into applied investigation and convert applied investigation into practice. This conference will also create awareness about the importance of basic scientific research in different fields matching with the current trends. The conference will provide the flavor of keynote lectures by eminent speakers from different areas and panel discussion by industry people. The scope of the conference includes all areas of Advanced Computing and Intelligent Engineering.

About Universite des Mascareignes(UDM)

Universite des Mascareignes is the fourth public University in Mauritius which was founded when the Institut Superieur de Technologie at Rose-Hill and Swami Dayanand Institute of Management at Pamplemousses were merged. Universite des Mascareignes provides tertiary education in Engineering, Sustainable Development, Banking and Finance, Management and Information Technology. It has a close working collaboration with the Universite de Limoges. The Universite des Mascareignes currently has 1,325 students who are enrolled in different programmes at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels.
